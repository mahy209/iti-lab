Grandma's Lemon Meringue pie

        one 9 inch pie 
        30 min - prep time 
        10 min -cook time
        40 min - total
        8 servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup of white suger
  • 2 tablespoons-all purpose-flour
  • 3 tablespoon starch
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 lemon, jucied
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 eggs yolk, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pir crust, baked
  • 4 eggs white
  • 6 tablespoons white-sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. preheat the ovan: 360 degree F
  2. make lemon filled: in medium of sausespan..
    • whiske together 1 cup of water, flour and slat
    • stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest
    • stir in butter
    • place eggs yolks in small bowls
    • pour filling to pastry shell
  3. make maringue: in a large glass or metal bowl...
    • whip egg white until foaming
    • add sugar gradually
    • spread marigue over pie
  4. bake: bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes
